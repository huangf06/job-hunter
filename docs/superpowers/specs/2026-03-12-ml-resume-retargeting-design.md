# ML Resume Retargeting Design

**Goal:** Reposition `templates/Fei_Huang_ML_Resume.svg` for standard ML Engineer roles without changing layout, by sharpening the narrative around decision systems, evaluation rigor, and production data foundations.

## Scope

- Content only
- No visual/layout changes
- No fabricated achievements or unsupported tools

## Design

The resume will keep `ML Engineer` as the primary identity, but avoid reading like a generic production-ML-platform profile. The summary will emphasize production decision systems, rigorous evaluation, and scalable data foundations. GLP will be the primary proof point and rewritten to sound more concrete and ML-relevant without overstating modern MLOps ownership. BQ and Ele.me will be tightened so they support quantitative and experimentation credibility without hijacking the narrative.

The skills section will stay ATS-usable but will remove or soften items that create obvious credibility gaps relative to the bullets. Projects and education will continue to provide the strongest direct ML evidence, especially around benchmarking, ranking, and statistical validation.
