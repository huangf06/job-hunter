## ✅ 建议的故事重构

### 核心叙事: "从机构到独立再到学术深造"

你是一个对量化交易有**持续热情和自主学习能力**的人：

1. **机构起步** (2015-2017): 在 Baiquan 建立量化研究基础
2. **业务转型** (2017-2019): 在 GLP 拓展数据科学/风控能力
3. **独立验证** (2019-2023): 离开机构，独立验证自己的策略和理念
4. **学术升级** (2023-2025): 为了更上一层楼，系统性深造AI

### 为什么这段自雇经历是加分项

**对于自营交易公司 (Prop Trading) 来说**：
- ✅ 你有独立工作的能力 (prop trading 需要自主性)
- ✅ 你用自己的钱交易过 (理解真金白银的压力)
- ✅ 你持续学习4年 (自驱力证明)
- ✅ 你有系统性方法 (回测框架、风险管理)

**对比普通候选人**:
- 大多数人只有机构经验，没有独立验证过自己
- 你有"离开机构也能生存"的自信和能力

## 🎤 面试时的讲故事框架

### 开场白 (30秒版本)

```
"I'm a quantitative researcher with 6 years of experience spanning 
institutional and independent settings.

I started at Baiquan Investment, a Beijing-based quant hedge fund, where 
I developed alpha research pipelines and live trading strategies.

Then I spent 4 years as an independent trader, building my own backtesting 
infrastructure and testing strategies with real capital. This period taught 
me a lot about disciplined risk management and the psychological aspects 
of trading.

Realizing I wanted to deepen my technical skills, I pursued an MSc in AI 
at VU Amsterdam, where I focused on uncertainty quantification in deep 
reinforcement learning - highly relevant to algorithmic trading.

Now I'm looking to bring this combined experience to a professional 
prop trading environment like Maverick."
```

### 应对"为什么离开机构"的问题

```
"After GLP, I wanted to test whether I could succeed independently. 
I had ideas I wanted to validate without institutional constraints.

It was a calculated risk - I gave myself a runway to see if my strategies 
worked in live markets. I built the full infrastructure: data pipelines, 
backtesting, execution simulations.

The experience gave me deeper respect for risk management and the 
discipline required in trading. It also showed me that while I can 
work independently, I thrive in collaborative environments with 
access to better data and infrastructure - which is why I'm now 
looking to join a firm like Maverick."
```

### 应对"这4年赚了多少钱"的问题

```
"I was trading with relatively small capital - this was more about 
validation than income generation. I focused on:

1. Building robust infrastructure
2. Developing and testing strategies
3. Learning what works and what doesn't

The goal was always to eventually return to an institutional setting 
with better tools and data - which led me to pursue the MSc and 
prepare for this transition."
```

## ⚠️ 避免的坑

### ❌ 不要说
- "我当时在休息/迷茫" → 听起来像借口
- "我没找到工作" → 显得被动
- "我只是随便试试" → 显得不专业

### ✅ 要说
- "我主动选择独立验证" → 展现自信
- "我系统化地构建交易体系" → 展现专业性
- "这让我更清楚自己想要什么" → 展现成熟度

## 🔥 总结: 怎么圆这个故事

**核心逻辑**: 你不是"有4年空白期"，你是"有4年宝贵的独立交易经验"。

**关键点**:
1. 把 Independent Investor 当作正式经历来讲，不是脚注
2. 强调这段时间的**系统性** (回测框架、风险管理)
3. 连接到你的**动机** (测试自己、深化理解)
4. 说明**为什么现在回来** (寻求更好平台、协作环境)

**底线**: 6年量化相关经验是真实的，关键是**怎么讲**这个故事。
