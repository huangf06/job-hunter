# 简历 vs Bullet Library 对比分析

## 🚨 发现的问题

### 1. 年限夸大 - 最严重问题

**简历声称**: "Quantitative Researcher with **6 years of experience**"

**实际情况**:
- Baiquan Investment: Jul 2015 - Jun 2017 = **2年** Quantitative Researcher
- GLP Technology: Jul 2017 - Aug 2019 = Data Scientist (信用风险，非量化)
- Ele.me: Sep 2013 - Jul 2015 = Data Analyst (非量化)
- 2019-2023: Career Transition (4年空白期)

**结论**: 你实际只有 **2年量化经验**，不是6年！

---

### 2. "derivatives pricing and automated trading systems" 夸大

**简历声称**: 具备衍生品定价和自动化交易系统经验

**实际情况**:
- Deribit Options System: Oct 2025 - Present (个人项目)
- **明确写了 "currently in paper-trading validation" (纸面交易验证中)**
- 不是生产系统，没有实盘经验

**问题**: 说"automated trading systems"暗示生产经验，但实际只是 side project

---

### 3. Career Gap 轻描淡写

**简历写法**: "2019-2023 included independent investing, language learning..."

**实际情况**:
- 这是 **4年的空白期**
- independent investor 的 bullets 包括:
  - "personal trading" (小资金实盘)
  - "grad prep" (申请学校)
  - "language learning"
  - "philosophy"

**问题**: 4年 gap 被一句话带过，面试官深挖时会暴露

---

### 4. 技能列表可能过于乐观

**列出**: 
- "Options Pricing, Black-Scholes, Greeks Computation, IV Solver"
- "Risk Analytics, Position Sizing, Drawdown Control"

**实际情况**:
- 这些都来自 **side project**，不是工作经验
- 没有实盘期权交易经验
- 没有机构的衍生品风险管理经验

---

## ✅ 如何圆回来 (面试策略)

### 策略1: 重新定义 "6 years of experience"

**如果被问到**: "You said 6 years, but Baiquan was only 2 years?"

**回答**:
```
"Let me clarify - I have 2 years of professional quantitative research 
experience at Baiquan Investment, plus 4 years of self-directed study 
and independent trading during my career transition period (2019-2023). 

During that time, I built systematic backtesting frameworks and 
developed event-driven trading strategies. While not institutional 
experience, it was intensive hands-on practice that prepared me for 
this role.

I should have been more precise in the resume wording."
```

---

### 策略2: 解释 "automated trading systems"

**如果被深挖 Deribit 项目**:

**回答**:
```
"The Deribit options system is a personal project I'm currently 
developing - it's in paper-trading validation phase, not production. 

I've implemented Black-Scholes pricing, Greeks calculations, and 
risk management frameworks, but I want to be transparent that this 
is not live trading experience.

My actual live trading experience was at Baiquan with the R-Breaker 
strategy, and during my independent period with small-capital equity 
strategies."
```

---

### 策略3: 正面应对 Career Gap

**如果被问到 2019-2023**:

**回答**:
```
"I took a 4-year career break starting in 2019. The first couple 
years were focused on personal trading and preparing for graduate 
studies abroad - IELTS, applications, and eventually admission to 
VU Amsterdam.

I also used this time for intensive self-study in quantitative 
finance, machine learning, and to improve my English. While not 
formal employment, I treated it as a full-time learning period.

I started my MSc in AI at VU Amsterdam in September 2023, which 
brought me back to formal education and research."
```

## 🔥 面试中的核心话术

### 开场就主动澄清 (推荐!)

与其等 TJ 发现，不如主动说明:

```
"TJ, before we dive in, I want to clarify something on my resume. 
It says '6 years of quantitative experience,' but I want to be 
transparent:

- 2 years was professional at Baiquan Investment
- 4 years was my career break where I did independent trading 
  and prepared for grad school

Also, the options trading system listed is a personal project 
in paper-trading phase, not production experience.

I believe my actual experience and recent MSc in AI make me a 
strong candidate, and I wanted to be upfront about this."
```

**为什么这样好**:
1. 展现诚实和透明度
2. 抢回主动权
3. TJ 可能会欣赏你的坦诚

---

## ⚠️ 风险评估

### 如果 TJ 发现你夸大
- 可能会质疑你的诚信
- 可能直接 pass
- 阿姆斯特丹量化圈小，影响声誉

### 如果你主动澄清
- 展现诚实 (量化圈很看重)
- 可能 still get 下一轮面试
- 至少不会留下坏印象

---

## 📝 总结: 面试时怎么圆

1. **主动澄清年限**: 2年专业 + 4年自学，不是6年专业
2. **明确 Deribit 是个人项目**: 纸面交易，不是实盘
3. **正面应对 career gap**: 4年休息，用于学习和准备留学
4. **强调实际优势**: Baiquan 的实盘经验 + VU 的 MSc + 自学能力

**底线**: 诚实是最好的策略，量化圈很看重 integrity。
