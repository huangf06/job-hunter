## Job Description

At ENPICOM we sit at the intersection of biology, software engineering, and AI. We build a digital platform that enables pharma and biotech companies to develop breakthrough medicine faster, and we're looking for engineers who want to make that happen.

Our platform processes trillions of sequencing reads, makes complex immunogenomics data usable for lab scientists, and integrates AI/ML so they don't need to write code themselves. Our customers include multiple Top 10 pharma companies, and the software we ship has a direct line to real-world drug discovery.

We're looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our engineering team in 's-Hertogenbosch. You'll work on hard problems (petabyte-scale data processing, ML integration, enterprise-grade reliability) alongside immunologists, ML researchers, and fellow engineers who hold each other to a high bar. With ~30 people and 84% of us in R&D, there's no layers of management that slow you down. You'll be working on core architecture, influence product direction, and ship things that actually get used.

## Qualifications
- Strong proficiency in **TypeScript, Python, React & SQL** (and ideally, **Rust**)
- Experience building and operating **cloud-native applications** (AWS preferred)
- Comfortable working with **containerized architectures** (Kubernetes, Docker)
- Solid understanding of **API design, scalable architecture, and data-intensive systems**
- You take ownership: from design through deployment through keeping it running
- Experience in or curiosity about the **life sciences / biotech** domain is a plus, not a must
- Effective communicator in an interdisciplinary, international team
- Based in the Netherlands or willing to work **hybrid** (2 days per week at the office) from our 's-Hertogenbosch office
