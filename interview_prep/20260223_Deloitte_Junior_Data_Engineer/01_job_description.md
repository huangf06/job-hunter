## Job Description

### About the role

Part of the **Platform Development & Integration** team within Deloitte Engineering. Working on projects to turn data into actionable insights, modernize data/cloud infrastructures, and help organizations become data-driven.

### Responsibilities

- Analyzing client needs and translating them into technical solutions using Apache Spark, Delta Lake, Hudi, PySpark, Spark SQL
- Designing, developing, and maintaining scalable data-driven solutions
- Building robust ETL and ELT data pipelines on AWS, Azure, GCP, Databricks, Snowflake
- Collaborating with senior engineers on data pipelines, architectures, and modeling
- Participating in team training sessions

### Requirements

- Technical Master's degree (Data Science, Data Engineering, AI, CS, Software Engineering, Statistics), OR Bachelor's + 2 years experience
- Basic knowledge of data pipelines using Spark, Airflow, Iceberg, Delta Lake, Hudi
- At least one programming language: Python or SQL (PySpark is a plus)
- Familiarity with AWS, Azure, GCP
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- **Excellent command of English AND Dutch (written and spoken)**
