# FareHarbor - Company Quick Facts

## Company Overview
- **Founded**: 2013 in Hawaii
- **Acquired by**: Booking Holdings (2018)
- **Industry**: Travel & Tourism SaaS
- **Product**: Booking and management software for tour/activity operators
- **Clients**: 20,000+ across 90+ countries
- **Team Size**: 700+ employees globally
- **Market Position**: Largest in the tours/activities booking space

## What They Do
FareHarbor provides software for experience providers (boat rentals, museums, food tours, events) to:
- Manage bookings and reservations
- Process payments
- Handle scheduling and capacity
- Optimize pricing and revenue
